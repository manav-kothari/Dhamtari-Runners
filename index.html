Welcome to Dhamtari Runners. Historical City of Dhamtari, our hometown, abbreviated from “Dhamma”+”Tarai” is situated in the fertile plains of Chhattisgarh Region. Mahanadi and its tributaries Sendur, Pairy, Sondur, Joan, Kharun and Shivnath run through the region. The fertility of lands of Dhamtari district can be attributed to the presence of these rivers. But not just that, they also attribute to the unexplored beauty that the Dhamtari region has.
Dhamtari Runners is a group of nature lovers and fitness enthusiasts, who believe in the therapy of running. We like exploring the areas around the city with our small runs, in small groups and sometimes alone! We believe the feeling that you get from a good run, small or long, is priceless, we run for life!!!

About Us

There is something magical about running: after a certain distance it transcends the body. Then a bit further, it transcends the mind. A bit further yet, and what you have before you, laid bare, is the soul.
In 2016, few people among us were running regularly on treadmills in our homes for 10-15 min, we realized that we were a little bored indoors with no inspiration and nature. We started running outdoors from one tree to another lamp post for 2-3 km. Slowly we increased our distance to 5kms, 6kms, and 10 kms and suddenly dhamtari's neighborhood was at our disposal. We realized the potential our hometown has. Trails around Rudri Barrage and Gangrel Dam became our favorite routes. We learnt about the Half Marathon around us and thought we could try our strength for 21 km and started practicing and within 4 months, we ran our first half marathon.
मैं अकेला ही चला था जानिब-ए-मंज़िल मगर लोग साथ आते गए और कारवाँ बनता गया. We had more people joining our group runs and dhamtari runners are now a group of 100+ like minded friends. We have been actively participating in multiple events for the last 3 years - Let's Run Half Marathon event, Rajnandgaon Half marathon, Nagpur Half Marathon, Airtel New Delhi Half Marathon, TATA MUMBAI FULL MARATHON, LADAKH FULL MARATHON to name the few.
We are the first group of our kind in the Dhamtari Area. Our runs started with the objective of gaining good health and now it has become the best way to spend time outdoors with nature and friends. We aim to create a culture of running in our beautiful 'Dhamtari' and reach new avenues. We are an open community for all and invite everyone to join our group and enjoy the activity of running and staying FIT AND HEALTHY.
